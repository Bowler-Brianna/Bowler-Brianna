Light in the Tunnel

Light in the Tunnel is an aspiring Addiction Recovery program for those who struggle with addiction, especially sex or pornography addiction. This program includes around the clock therapists to listen, rooms available for those who are seperated from spouses or loved ones, a loving staff, and a safe environement. The recovery program is 6 months with intensive care, and then a year of bi-weekly mettings, and a lifetime of love, support, and encouragement.

Results

Like any addiction recovery program, self will, determination, and patience are key. To see results you will need to be commited, focused, and doing this for the right reasons. Being clean for a week isn't the goal- a lifetime of soberiety is. This program will be based off of the 12 step program, which has seen amazing results for decades! we can guarantee that if you follow this program, and each step with your whole heart, and intent to change you will succeed and see the results you and your future generations could hope for.
